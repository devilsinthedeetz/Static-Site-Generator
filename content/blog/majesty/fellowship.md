# The Fellowship of the Ring

In the fading years of the Third Age, **Frodo Baggins** inherited a burden that was both _small in size_ and **terrible in _power_**.

The inscription upon the Ring read:

> ## The One Ring
>
> Three Rings for the Elven-kings under the sky,
> Seven for the Dwarf-lords in their halls of stone,
> Nine for Mortal Men doomed to die,
> One for the Dark Lord on his dark throne.
>
> - **One Ring** to rule them all
> - **One Ring** to find them
> - **One Ring** to bring them all
> - And in the darkness _bind them_

## Members of the Fellowship

The Fellowship included warriors, wanderers, and unlikely heroes. Some carried swords, while others carried little more than courage and a good supply of lembas.

| Name | Weapon or Gift | Fate |
| :--- | :---: | ---: |
| Frodo Baggins | _Sting_ and the Phial | Ring-bearer |
| Samwise Gamgee | Loyalty and a frying pan | **The _true_ hero** |
| Aragorn | _Andúril_ | King of Gondor |
| Legolas | Bow of the Galadhrim | Walks lightly over snow |
| Gimli | Battle-axe | Friend of an Elf |
| Gandalf | Glamdring and wisdom | Returns as **Gandalf the _White_** |

## The Road from the Shire

1. Leave Bag End before the Black Riders arrive
2. Meet **Strider** at the _Prancing Pony_
3. Reach Rivendell and attend the Council of Elrond
4. Attempt the pass of `Caradhras`
5. Enter the Mines of Moria
6. Escape across the Bridge of Khazad-dûm
7. Rest in Lothlórien
8. Travel south upon the Anduin
9. Break the Fellowship at Amon Hen

## Supplies Packed by Sam

- A coil of rope from Lothlórien
- Several packets of _lembas_
- Cooking gear, including his **most important _frying pan_**
- A small box of salt from the Shire
- Hope, even when Frodo had almost none

## At the Black Gate

The captains of the West did not expect victory. Their purpose was to draw the Eye away from two small figures crossing the plain of Gorgoroth.

> ### Aragorn's Challenge
>
> **Sons of Gondor! Of Rohan! My brothers!**
>
> A day may come when the courage of Men fails,
> when we forsake our friends and break all bonds of fellowship,
> but it is not this day.
>
> 1. Stand before the Black Gate
> 2. Hold the line
> 3. Trust that _Frodo and Sam_ are still moving

## Final Accounting

| Quest | Bearer | Result |
| --- | :---: | ---: |
| Carry the Ring from the Shire | Frodo | Completed |
| Protect Frodo | Sam | **Completed beyond measure** |
| Defeat Sauron by force | The West | Impossible |
| Destroy the Ring | Gollum's final fall | Achieved |

The quest succeeded not because the heroes were flawless, but because mercy shown long before became decisive at the end.

**Even the smallest person can change the course of the _future_.**
